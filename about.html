About KANATAL PINNACLE HOUSE

This property has views of the Himalayan range including the peaks of Gangotri’s snow views. It is located at app. 8900 ft above sea level and has one of the most popular temple’s named Surkanda Devi. This temple is dedicated to the Goddess SATI; it is a Hindu Shakti Peeth and is located at app. 10000 ft height above sea level.

PINNACLE HOUSE is about 300kms from Delhi and is possibly the nearest unexplored snow covered tourist destination from the Capital City.

FACILITIES: 24 hrs Front-desk service along with Wi-Fi. The hotel has a combination of Deluxe Rooms, Super Deluxe Rooms and Suites. Parking is available

ACTIVITIES: There are a variety of exciting indoor activities available for our guests such as Billiards, Carrom etc. Exercise Machines, Yoga Centre and a Large-Hall area for recreation is also available.

ATTRACTIONS: KANATAL Adventure Sports Activities, Surkanda Devi Temple, Tehri Dame, Kodia Jungle Safari, Dhanaulti Eco Park.

TIME TO VISIT: Between December to February when the snow carpets all the surrounding areas making Kanatal look no less than a paradise on Earth.

TO REACH BY ROAD: Dehradun – Mussoorie, Dhanaulti, Kanatal and by Rishikesh, Narender Nagar, Chamba, Mussoorie Road, Kanatal. Distance from Dehradun to Kanatal – 75 Kms approx. Distance from Rishikesh to Kanatal – 80 Kms approx.

TO REACH BY TRAIN: The nearest Railway Station is Haridwar/ Dehradun. BY FLIGHT: Jolly Grand Dehradun Airport and then go to by Road Narender Nagar – Chamba – Kanatal.
